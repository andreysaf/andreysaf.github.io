FAQ

Dresscode

Semi-formal suggested for the ceremony and reception. Casual for all other events. Please refrain from wearing a red qipao.

Gifts

We do not have a registry or honeymoon fund. Your presence and well wishes are present enough. Sentimental cards will be gratefully accepted.

Accomodation

Accommodation at or near the venue will be on us, for the nights before and after the wedding.

Getting there

Canmore is on Highway 1, about an hour west of Calgary. Driving from Vancouver necessitates crossing several mountain passes which frequently close due to snowstorms, etc. If you are not experienced with winter conditions, we recommend first flying to Calgary. From the Calgary Airport (YYC), you may rent a car or take a bus/shuttle to Canmore. If you have difficulty arranging transportation, please contact us for assistance.

What to pack

Most importantly - warm winter clothing and footwear! You may also wish to bring a bathing suit for the outdoor hot tub.

Photos

You are welcome to take photos anytime you like, however, we ask that you please avoid obstructing our professional photographers, Cat and Alex.

Perfume

Please refrain from use of heavily scented products.
